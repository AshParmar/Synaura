Bioaerosols may contain pathogenic microbiologic agents, such as those that can cause Legionnaires’ disease, viral pneumonia, and the common cold, as well as allergens derived from pet dander, dust mites, and fungi and molds, which can cause rhinitis, eye irritation, and even asthma.


Another serious consequence of the lack of vitamin A is immune deficiency. This impairment of immunity leads to higher mortality rates from common infections such as measles, pneumonia, and infectious diarrhea. In parts of the world with a high prevalence of vitamin A deficiency, dietary supplements reduce mortality rates for infectious disorders by 20% to 30%.
